Featured Trips

Rishikesh Rafting

Embark on an epic journey through the heart of Rishikesh's stunning landscapes. This expedition offers a perfect blend of exhilarating rapids, breathtaking scenery, and cultural immersion. Navigate the challenging waters, camp under the stars, and explore the rich heritage of Peru. This trip is designed for experienced rafters seeking an unforgettable adventure.

River Family Adventure

Experience the thrill of whitewater rafting with your family on the picturesque Yamuna River. This adventure is perfect for beginners and families, offering a mix of calm stretches and exciting rapids suitable for all ages. Enjoy camping by the river, swimming in pristine waters, and creating lasting memories with your loved ones.

Ganga River Explorer's Quest

Dive into the ultimate rafting challenge on the legendary Ganga River. This expedition is for expert rafters looking to test their skills on some of the most intense rapids in the world. Navigate through breathtaking gorges, conquer adrenaline-pumping rapids, and soak in the beauty of the Ganga's surroundings. This trip promises an adventure of a lifetime for thrill-seekers.
