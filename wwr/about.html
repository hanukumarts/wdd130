History

Our rafting company was founded in 2022 by a group of passionate river guides with a love for adventure. Since then, we have been dedicated to providing thrilling and memorable rafting experiences for adventurers of all ages. Over the years, we have expanded our offerings to include a variety of trips, from scenic floats for beginners to challenging rapids for experienced rafters. Our commitment to safety, fun, and environmental stewardship has made us a leader in the rafting industry.
